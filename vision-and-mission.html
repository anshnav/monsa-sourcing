Vision & Mission

Our Vision

A preferred sourcing partner for those who believe in leaving a mark

To be the preferred sourcing partner for global and domestic brands and retailers by providing exceptional service, value additions, market intelligence and process mapping for efficient sourcing.

Enable our partners to build and execute a range of products suited to their target market, incorporating the required design sensibilities sourced at the optimal costs. Add value and provide actionable information at every stage for our partners to make informed decisions.

To curate, and help create an authentic yet innovative product, using a perfect blend of tradition and contemporary style, be it for home, apparel or accessories.

Our Mission

Making Ideas Happen

We are dedicated towards innovating and creating newness and inspiring our global partners to see the beauty and magic in all that we have to offer.

Our mission is to offer the Indian capabilities to international markets – an ever emerging sourcing destination.

Provide end2end sourcing solutions in design, product development, cost negotiation, manufacturing, quality and training vendor partners.

To build and deliver credible business model by following the ‘5 Ps” of sustainable development goals: People, Planet, Prosperity, Peace and Partnership.
