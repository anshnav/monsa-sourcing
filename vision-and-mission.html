Our vision and mission

Our Vision

A preferred sourcing partner for those who believe in leaving a mark.

The Crafts of the world are diverse and rich in history. Each one of them have a story to tell; a story of tradition, a story of migration, a story of conquest or a story of perseverance.

Be it the Rug Makers of Turkey, Flamenco Guitar Luthiers of Madrid, the Glass Blowers of Murano or the Kaarigars of India, each have a unique history worth preserving.

India is steeped in these traditions from the Pashmina shawls from Kashmir, zardozi and chikankaari from Lucknow, Ajrakh prints and Shibori from Rajasthan, Palm leaf Basketry from Tamil Nadu, Wooden toys from Channapatna, the list is never ending!

Indian handicraft is a sleeping giant. With more than 3000 craft forms (and counting) it’s a land of creative wonders.

Mass production gives us great value, but the uniqueness of that hand crafted rug, or that hand printed table line, or that hand embroidered dress, keeps alive the story of a tradition that has survived the tides of time or a culture that withstood centuries of regimes. A timeless, priceless creation!

We, at MonSa, strive to revive these timeless pieces, blend them a bit with a modern touch and showcase them to the World.

We curate, and help create an authentic yet innovative product, using a perfect blend of tradition and contemporary style, be it for home, apparel or accessories.

Our Mission

Making Ideas Happen

We are dedicated towards innovating and creating newness and inspiring our global partners to see the beauty and magic in all that we have to offer.

Our mission is to tour the Indian handcraft to international markets – an unrealized potential.

Provide end2end sourcing solutions in design, product development, cost negotiation, manufacturing, quality and training vendor partners.

To build and deliver credible business model by following the ‘5 Ps” of sustainable development goals: People, Planet, Prosperity, Peace and Partnership.
